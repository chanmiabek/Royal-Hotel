About Royal Hotel

Established in 1995, Royal Hotel has been the premier destination for travelers seeking a perfect blend of luxury, comfort, and heritage.

A Legacy of Hospitality

For over nearly three decades, we have defined the standard for luxury lodging. What started as a small boutique inn has grown into a world-renowned landmark, known for its iconic architecture and unwavering commitment to guest satisfaction.

Every brick in our building tells a story of elegance. We pride ourselves on maintaining a classic atmosphere while integrating the latest in smart-hotel technology.

Fine Dining & Culinary Art

Our award-winning restaurant, *The Royal Palate*, offers an exquisite menu crafted by Michelin-starred chefs. We source all our ingredients from local organic farms to ensure every bite is fresh and flavorful.

From our signature breakfast buffet to intimate candlelit dinners, we provide a gastronomic journey that satisfies the most discerning palates.

Wellness & Serenity

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city in our full-service spa and wellness center. We offer a range of treatments, including Swedish massages, rejuvenating facials, and a heated indoor swimming pool.

Our state-of-the-art fitness center is open 24/7, allowing you to maintain your routine while enjoying panoramic views of the city skyline.
